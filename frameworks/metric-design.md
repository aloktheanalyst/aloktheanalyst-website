---
id: metric-design
name: Metric Design / KPI Framework
badge: "AARRR / North Star Metric"
category: analytics

coaching:
  use_for: "'design KPIs for feature X', 'what metrics would you track?', 'how would you measure success?'"
  steps:
    - title: IDENTIFY THE GOAL
      prompt: |
        What is the goal of the feature/product? (acquisition, activation, retention, revenue, referral)
    - title: PRIMARY METRIC (NORTH STAR)
      prompt: |
        One number that captures success
    - title: SECONDARY / GUARDRAIL METRICS
      prompt: |
        What shouldn't get worse
    - title: LEADING VS LAGGING INDICATORS
      prompt: |
        Leading indicators predict future performance; lagging confirm past results
    - title: SET TARGETS
      prompt: |
        How to set targets — benchmarks, historical data, cohort comparison
    - title: SEGMENTATION
      prompt: |
        By user type, geography, platform, cohort
